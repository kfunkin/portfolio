MA Project Live Brief: AR Photo Booth Augmented Reality App (UI & UX Design)

The aim of the project was to produce a working prototype of an augmented reality app that would act as an alternative to a novelty photo booth, to be used at weddings and functions. Unity, Illustrator and Photoshop were used to create the UI for this project.

A tool to enhance the event experience utilizing AR to produce a memento of a special day
