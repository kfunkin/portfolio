MA Project: App As A Format To Release Music

The aim of the project was to produce an app-album, exploring the feasibility of music being released as an app, based on a fictional funk band from Russia called French Fries. The app-album features original music and an interactive story, based on research on the subject of app-albums and music consumption. The project started as an observation of a lack of visual content in streaming. Made with Illustrator, Photoshop, HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
